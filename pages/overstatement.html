Overstatement vs a Fish Story


  • Fish Story: an exaggerated story with the intent of deceiving the audience from true events
  • Overstatement: merely stretches the truth in order to make the true meaning more clear


Purpose of Overstatement in Literature & Poetry

  • Humor
  • More Impact for a Theme or Message
  • Compare & Connect
