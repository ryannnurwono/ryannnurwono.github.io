--- layout: page title: Paradox ---

Paradox - something that seems like a contradiction, but is true in one way or another

Example: "All animals are equal, but some are more equal than others" - Animal Farm


Logical Paradox vs Literary Paradox


  • Logical Paradox: a contradiction that defies logic and seems unsolvable
  • Literary Paradox: a statement that contradicts itself in order to shock the reader and reveal deeper meaning and thought


Purpose of a Paradox in Literature

  • Humor
  • Develop Theme
  • Provoke Thought
